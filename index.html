Track The Test

Track the Test is a strong Analytical Test Tool developed by combining unique data capturing and mining based on Artificial Intelligence, Data Science Algorithms and Predictive Learning Techniques to enable every test conducting organization to conduct examination in a more Structured and Effective manner. The Solution has been developed to ensure End to End Governance at all the stages of the examination covering all the stakeholders including Candidates, Invigilators, Observers, Agency and their staff and any other support staff deployed during the examination.

Offerings
  • Platform to streamline examination systems in India
  • Pre approval methods and predictability on testing outcomes
  • Free services on centre wise infra reports
  • Analytics and report on chargeable basis
